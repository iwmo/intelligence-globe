## Decisions Made
- **ts coercion:** Test sends `1_700_000_000_000` (Unix ms integer), not ISO string. Added `field_validator("ts")` in `OsintEventCreate` that converts ms epoch to `datetime` before DB write.
- **Category validation in Pydantic:** Used `field_validator("category")` raising `ValueError` rather than a route-level `HTTPException` — FastAPI automatically converts Pydantic `ValueError` to 422, matching test assertion.
- **Migration cleanup:** Autogenerate included a spurious `op.drop_index('idx_aircraft_latlon_not_null')` diff. Removed it from the migration to keep the change scoped to osint_events creation only.
